Attack on Titan

Witness the struggle for survival as humanity battles giant humanoid creatures known as Titans. "Attack on Titan" delivers intense action, complex characters, and a gripping storyline.

One Piece

Embark on a grand adventure with Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew in search of the legendary treasure, One Piece. This long-running series combines humor, friendship, and epic battles.

Take a journey through time with Takemichi Hanagaki as he tries to change the future and save his friends. "Tokyo Revengers" combines gang conflicts and time-travel elements.
